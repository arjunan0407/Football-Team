C Ronaldo

Forward

"Cristiano Ronaldo" is a Portuguese professional footballer widely regarded as one of the greatest players in history. Born on February 5, 1985, he captains both Saudi Pro League club Al-Nassr and the Portugal national team, having built a legendary career defined by over 900 official career goals and five Ballon d'Or awards.

L Messi

Forward

"Lionel Andrés Messi" is an Argentine professional footballer broadly considered one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. Widely celebrated for his dazzling dribbling, precise passing, and prolific goal-scoring, he captains both Major League Soccer's Inter Miami and the Argentina national team.

Neymar JR

Forward

"Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior" universally known as Neymar Jr., is a Brazilian professional footballer widely regarded as one of the most skillful, technically gifted, and creative forwards of his generation. He is renowned for his flair, dribbling ability, and proficiency in scoring with both feet.
